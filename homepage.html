About FarmDirect

Our Story

FarmDirect was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to connect consumers directly with local farmers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices for both farmers and customers.

Our Values

  • Supporting local farmers and sustainable agriculture
  • Providing fresh, high-quality products
  • Transparent pricing and fair trade
  • Reducing food miles and carbon footprint

Our Team

We're a small team of passionate individuals committed to revolutionizing the way people access fresh, local produce. Our team includes agricultural experts, tech enthusiasts, and sustainability advocates working together to build a better food system.
